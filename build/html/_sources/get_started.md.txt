### Running Your Game
Make sure your {doc}`ScriptDoc/file_structure` is correctly organized, with your `bieg2D` app next to your `resources` folder.
#### MacOS
Open the folder containing the engine and `resources` in your terminal, and run it with `./game_engine`.
```
$ ls
game_engine             resources
$ ./game_engine
```
