Running Your Game¶

Make sure your File Structure is correctly organized, with your bieg2D app next to your resources folder.

MacOS¶

Open the folder containing the engine and resources in your terminal, and run it with ./game_engine.

$ ls
game_engine             resources
$ ./game_engine
